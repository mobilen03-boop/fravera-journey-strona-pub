Our Mission

Why we exist

Poland holds some of the largest mineral water and peat resources in Europe, with a spa treatment tradition dating back to the 19th century. We believe this heritage deserves recognition in the European wellness market.

01

Cultural Bridge

We understand Western European expectations regarding quality, transparency and service standards. We translate these requirements into the Polish context.

02

Transparent Model

As a disclosed agent, we ensure clients pay directly to the resort. Our agency commission comes from the provider, not from inflating prices.

03

Quality Curation

We personally verify selected properties to ensure they meet the expectations of clients accustomed to high standards. Not every resort qualifies.

Founder

Radosław

Lead Coordinator & Market Connector

FraVeRa Journey emerged from the need to connect two worlds I know from personal experience — the Western European market and Polish health resorts.

Living and working in Western Europe, I see high expectations regarding quality, transparency, and service standards. At the same time, I know that Poland possesses unique natural resources, qualified medical staff, and spa infrastructure that in many aspects rivals renowned resorts in Germany, Austria, or Switzerland.

My goal is not selling stays. My goal is building a bridge of trust — so that a client from the Netherlands, Belgium, or Germany can safely, consciously, and comfortably benefit from the potential of Polish health resorts.

In working with individual and institutional clients, understanding cultural differences, communication standards, and organizational expectations is crucial. Thanks to international work experience, I can anticipate these differences and eliminate the risk of misunderstandings.

"I don't want to convince anyone that Poland is cheaper. I want to show that it is equally good — and often more authentic."
