About Us

FraVeRa Journey

We are a registered travel agent acting as an intermediary between Western European clients and Polish health resorts. We do not own the properties — we coordinate the booking process and provide support at every stage.

Our model is based on transparency: the client pays directly to the resort, and we receive an agency commission. This ensures transaction security and the best price for you.

We specialize in clients from Dutch-speaking and German-speaking countries, understanding their specific expectations regarding service standards and communication.
