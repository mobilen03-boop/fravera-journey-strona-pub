§ 1. Nature of FraVeRa Journey's Business and Legal Role

1.1. Nature of Business

FraVeRa Journey conducts business consisting of intermediation in organizing tourist, health and related services, acting as an agent (intermediary) between the client and independent service providers.

Legal effect: FraVeRa Journey is not a party to contracts for main services (accommodation, treatment, transport).

1.2. No Tour Operator Status

FraVeRa Journey does not combine tourist services into packages and does not offer them at a single lump-sum price.

Legal effect: FraVeRa Journey does not bear tour operator liability within the meaning of EU law.

§ 2. Payment Model

2.1. Deposit (Retainer)

After accepting the preliminary offer, the Client pays a non-refundable deposit of at least €120, which initiates the reservation process.

2.2. Collective Settlement Note

After finalizing arrangements, the Client receives a Collective Settlement Note containing FraVeRa's remuneration and amounts due to third parties.

2.3. T-21 Payment Deadline

Payment for the stay should be made no later than 21 days before arrival. For last-minute bookings (≤7 days), immediate payment is required.

§ 3. Liability

3.1. Limitation of Liability

FraVeRa Journey is not liable for: quality of services provided by third parties, treatment outcomes, or insurer reimbursement decisions.

§ 4. Cancellation and Refunds

Cancellation >30 days: full refund minus deposit. Cancellation 21-30 days: 50% refund. Cancellation <21 days: no refund.

§ 5. Final Provisions

Contracts are governed by the law of the Kingdom of the Netherlands and EU law. Disputes shall be resolved by courts competent for FraVeRa Journey's registered office.
