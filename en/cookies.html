Cookie Policy

§ 1. General Information

This Cookie Policy sets out the rules for using cookies and similar technologies on the website operated by the Data Controller.

Legal basis: Article 5(3) of Directive 2002/58/EC (ePrivacy)

§ 2. Definition of Cookies

Cookies are IT data stored on the user's end device, used to ensure proper website functioning, traffic analysis and marketing activities.

§ 3. Legal Basis for Using Cookies

The use of cookies is in accordance with GDPR and ePrivacy Directive regulations. Non-essential cookies are used only after obtaining user consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

§ 4. Necessary (Technical) Cookies

Necessary cookies are used solely to ensure website functioning and do not require user consent.

§ 5. Analytical Cookies (Google Analytics 4)

The Controller uses Google Analytics 4 analytical cookies for statistical analysis of website usage, only after obtaining user consent.

§ 6. Marketing and Remarketing Cookies

Marketing cookies are used for advertising campaigns, remarketing and measuring ad effectiveness. They are used only after obtaining user consent.

§ 7. Google Consent Mode v2

The Controller uses Google Consent Mode v2, which enables sending consent signals to Google services for categories: ad_storage, analytics_storage, ad_user_data, ad_personalization.

§ 8. Consent Management Platform (CMP)

The Controller uses a Consent Management Platform that allows users to grant, refuse and withdraw cookie consent at any time.

§ 9. Withdrawing Consent and Changing Settings

Users can withdraw their consent or change cookie settings at any time via CMP or browser settings.

§ 10. Cookie Retention Period

Cookie retention period depends on their type: Session cookies — until browser is closed. Persistent cookies — from 30 days to 2 years.

§ 11. Third Parties

Cookies may be installed by third parties, in particular: Google (Analytics, Ads), Meta (Facebook, Instagram).

§ 12. Changes to Cookie Policy

The Controller reserves the right to change this Cookie Policy in case of changes in law or technologies used.

§ 13. Entry into Force

This Cookie Policy is effective from the date of its publication on the Controller's website.
